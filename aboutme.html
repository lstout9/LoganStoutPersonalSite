A little bit about me

Hello! I'm Logan Stout, currently interning at WebBank as a Financial Data Specialist. I'm a student at Utah State University, where I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems with an emphasis in Data Engineering, and I'm also pursuing a dual major in Finance. My dedication to both fields is reflected in my 3.7 GPA, even while working an average of 30 hours a week. I am excited to say that I will be completing my degrees in December!

My education has fueled my passion for data and programming, prompting me to explore a wide range of tools and technologies, including Python, SQL, Alteryx, Tableau, UiPath, Power BI, PowerApps, PowerAutomate, and Snowflake.

My previous experiences have been diverse and enriching. At Koch Agronomic and Energy Solutions, I worked as an Analyst, where I automated processes, developed applications for sales teams, and analyzed opportunity costs while contributing to the company's daily operations.

My dedication extends beyond the professional realm. I've also volunteered as a Full-Time Volunteer at The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the Dominican Republic. There, I honed my leadership skills and collaborated with various communities, serving people from diverse backgrounds and cultures.

My true passion lies in the world of data. I'm excited to continue my journey of skill development, in a career that will allow me to do so. I'm eager to collaborate with seasoned professionals and learn from their experiences. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website!
